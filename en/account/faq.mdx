## Do you have any questions?

Please check the frequently asked questions and answers below.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Are ClawOnPhone and ClawTalk different apps?">
    Yes, they are separate apps.

    **ClawOnPhone** is a voice AI assistant app. You can handle work by talking to it, and it manages core settings like Google account connection and PC remote control.

    **ClawTalk** is a text messenger. You can chat with colleagues or assign tasks to the AI bot Veta via text.

    Both apps are linked to the same Google account, so the Google services connected in ClawOnPhone can also be used by the Veta bot in ClawTalk.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title='Veta says "Not connected to Google Services"'>
    This message appears when a Google account hasn't been connected yet.

    Open the **ClawOnPhone app** and tap the **G button** on the screen to connect your Google account. Once connected, you can use Google features (Email, Calendar, Files, etc.) with the Veta bot in both ClawTalk and Telegram.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I'm not receiving notifications on my iPhone">
    On iPhones, you must **add the PWA app to your Home Screen** to receive notifications.

    Open ClawTalk in Safari, tap the Share button at the bottom, and select "Add to Home Screen". After that, please allow notifications for ClawTalk in your iPhone's Settings app.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I set up PC Remote Control?">
    Two things are required to use PC Remote Control.

    First, install the **ClawOnPhone PC Node App** on your company PC. Second, log in with the **same Google account** used in the ClawOnPhone mobile app.

    Once the connection is complete, you can issue commands from your mobile to Veta like, "Open the file on my PC" or "Show me the folder".
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Where is the conversation history saved?">
    All conversation records are safely encrypted and stored on a **Cloud Server**.

    Only you can access your conversation data, and all communication with the server is protected by encryption (HTTPS).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I use it for free?">
    Yes, you can use it for free with the Free Plan. You can make requests to Veta up to 10 times a day.

    If you need more features and usage time, you can upgrade to the Plus ($12.9/month) or Pro ($39/month) plans. Currently, during the beta period, some features might be provided for free.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I use all the features in Telegram as well?">
    Yes, you can use the exact same AI features in Telegram as in ClawTalk.

    All of Veta's tools—such as checking email, calendar, file search, and web search—are fully available in Telegram. However, to use Google-related tools, you must first connect your Google account in the ClawOnPhone app.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

If you have any further questions, ask Veta in ClawTalk or contact the administrator.
