## Modifying My Profile

In ClawTalk, you can change your display name, status message, and profile picture that other people see.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Settings tag">
    Tap the **Settings** tab at the bottom of the ClawTalk app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Edit Profile">
    Tap the profile area displayed at the top to move to the edit screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Change Profile Picture">
    Tap the profile picture area to select a photo from your gallery or take a new one with your camera.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Change Name">
    Tap the name input field to change your name. This name will appear in friends lists and chat rooms.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Status Message">
    You can enter a short message in the status message input field. Keeping a message like "Working outside" or "In a meeting" lets your colleagues know your current availability.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Once you're done editing, press the **Save** button to complete the process.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Modifiable Items

| Item | Description |
|------|------|
| **Profile Picture** | You can select a photo from your gallery or take a new one with the camera. |
| **Name** | This name is displayed in chat rooms and the friends list. |
| **Status Message** | You can leave a short message on your profile. |

<Tip>
  Your profile picture will be automatically resized to an appropriate size when uploaded. There is no need to worry even if you select a large image, as the resizing is automatic.
</Tip>

Changes made to your profile are immediately reflected on other users' friends lists and chat rooms.
