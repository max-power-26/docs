## Pricing Plans Guide

ClawOnPhone offers four pricing plans depending on your usage.

| Plan | Monthly Price | Key Benefits |
|--------|---------|-----------|
| **Free** | Free | 10 executions per day, Basic tools |
| **Plus** | $12.9 | 100 minutes per month, Full tool access |
| **Pro** | $39 | 300 minutes per month, Custom agent |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | Team management, Multi-PC connection |

## Free vs Plus: What's the Difference?

<CardGroup cols={2}>

<Card title="Free Plan" icon="gift">
  You can make requests to Veta up to **10 times** a day. You can experience basic tools like Web Search, Weather, and Task Management for free. Recommended for users trying ClawOnPhone for the first time.
</Card>

<Card title="Plus Plan" icon="star">
  Allows AI usage for up to **100 minutes** per month. You get access to **all Google tools**—such as Gmail, Google Calendar, and Google Drive—as well as PC Remote Control. Recommended for those who want to fully utilize ClawOnPhone for their work.
</Card>

</CardGroup>

## Pro Plan

The Pro Plan provides a generous **300 minutes** of usage per month and allows you to create **Custom Agents** tailored to your specific work patterns. Suitable for power users who want to fully automate their repetitive tasks.

## Enterprise Plan

If you plan to use it as a team, you can opt for the Enterprise Plan. This includes team management features, multi-PC connection capabilities, dedicated support, and more. Please contact us separately for more details.

## How to Upgrade

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the ClawOnPhone App">
    Please open the ClawOnPhone PWA app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to the Settings Menu">
    Please open the **Settings** menu on the screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a Plan">
    Your current plan and a list of available plans to upgrade to will be displayed. Please select the plan you desire.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the Payment">
    Enter your payment details, and the upgrade will be applied immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  During the current beta period, some features may be provided for free. The official pricing plans will apply after the beta ends.
</Note>
