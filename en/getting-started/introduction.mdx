## What is ClawOnPhone?

ClawOnPhone is an **AI agent that executes tasks**.

While AI like ChatGPT only tells you "how to do it," ClawOnPhone **actually executes it for you.** If you say, "Schedule a meeting for 10 AM tomorrow," it literally registers the event in your calendar. If you say, "Send the quote to Manager Kim," it actually sends the email.

You can handle repetitive tasks that used to take 40 minutes in **just 10 seconds**, automating your work with just one hand on your phone.

## ClawTalk Messenger

ClawTalk is a **business messenger** that is as easy to use as popular messengers.

You can chat in real-time with team members and clients, and delegate tasks to the AI bot Veta in the same chat room. While chatting, if you type "Veta, send an email with this content," it gets processed immediately.

There's no need to switch back and forth between different apps; you can communicate and handle work all in one place.

## Telegram Integration

If you already use Telegram, you can use ClawOnPhone AI within Telegram as well.

Send a message to the Veta Bot in a Telegram channel, and the AI will handle the work exactly like in ClawTalk. There is no need to switch your existing messenger.

ClawOnPhone is a service for **every professional whose time is taken up by repetitive tasks**.

- **Sales Professionals**: Check emails, manage schedules, and contact clients all at once via phone while on the move.
- **Planners**: Delegate document searching, meeting note organization, and schedule coordination to the AI, and focus on planning.
- **Managers**: Automate repetitive reporting, checking, and relaying tasks to increase management efficiency.

We help busy people focus on truly important work.

## Install Immediately without App Stores

ClawOnPhone is built as a **PWA (Progressive Web App)**.

Simply put, you can **install it directly from your internet browser** without needing to download it from the App Store or Play Store. After installation, you can launch it directly from your home screen just like a regular app.
