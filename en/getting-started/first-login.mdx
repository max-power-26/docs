## Login Preparation

ClawOnPhone requires a **Google Account** to log in. No separate sign-up is required, and if you have a Google Account used for Gmail or other services, you can start immediately.

<Tip>
  If your company uses Google Workspace (business Gmail), we recommend **logging in with your company Google Account**. This directly integrates with your company email, calendar, and drive, allowing you to fully utilize all ClawOnPhone features.
</Tip>

## How to Log In

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the App">
    Launch the installed ClawOnPhone or ClawTalk app. If you haven't installed them, you can also access them via a browser at the following URLs:

    - ClawOnPhone: https://alarm-e59f0.web.app
    - ClawTalk: https://url-saas-240311.web.app
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap the Google Login Button">
    You'll see a **"Sign in with Google"** button on the app screen. Please tap this button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Google Account">
    The Google login screen will appear. Select the Google account you wish to use, or enter your email address and password to log in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Login Complete">
    Once you select your account, you will automatically return to the app screen. If you see your name or profile picture at the top of the screen, the login is complete.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  A profile is automatically created upon your first login. Your Google account's name and photo are applied automatically, so no separate setup is needed.
</Info>

## You Only Need to Log In Once

Once you log in, your **login status is preserved** even if you close and reopen the app. Enjoy the convenience without needing to log in every time.

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What if I don't have a Google Account?">
    You can create a Google Account for free. From the Google search page (google.com), tap the "Sign in" button in the top right corner, then select "Create account" to set up a new account in just a few minutes.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I tapped the login button, but nothing happened">
    There could be a few causes for this.

    First, if your browser's pop-up blocker is turned on, the Google login window might not open. Disable the pop-up blocker and try again.

    Second, an outdated browser cache can cause issues. Clear your browser's cookies and cache, then retry. On iPhone Safari, you can clear this in "Settings > Safari > Clear History and Website Data".

    Third, your internet connection might be unstable. Please check your Wi-Fi or mobile data connection status.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I switch to a different account?">
    On the app screen, tap your profile area (name or photo) to find the logout button. After logging out, please log in again with the desired account.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Should I log in with my company account or my personal account?">
    If your company uses Google Workspace, we highly recommend using your company account. It grants direct access to your company's Gmail, Calendar, and Drive, making your work tasks much easier. If you are using it for personal purposes, logging in with your personal Gmail account is perfectly fine.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
