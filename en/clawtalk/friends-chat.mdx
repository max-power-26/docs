## Adding Friends

To start a conversation in ClawTalk, you must first add a friend.

<Steps>

<Step title="Go to the Search tab">
Tap the **Search** tab in the bottom navigation block.
</Step>

<Step title="Enter the email address">
Enter the email address of the colleague you want to add into the search bar. Only users registered on ClawTalk will be searched.
</Step>

<Step title="Tap the Add Friend button">
Once you find the desired user in the search results, tap the **Add Friend** button to add them to your friends list.
</Step>

</Steps>

<Note>
The other person must be registered on ClawTalk to appear in the search. If they are not registered, they will not show up in the search results.
</Note>

## Checking the Friends List

Tap the **Friends** tab in the bottom navigation to view your entire friends list.

The friends list is divided into two groups.

**Favorites**: Friends you contact frequently will appear at the top of the list if you add them to your favorites. Tap the star icon on a friend's profile to favorite them.

**Regular Friends**: Friends not added to your favorites will be displayed in the general list.

## Starting a 1:1 Chat

<Steps>

<Step title="Tap the friend you want to chat with from the Friends tab">
Tap the profile of the friend you want to talk to from your friends list.
</Step>

<Step title="Tap the 1:1 Chat button">
Tap the **1:1 Chat** button on the profile screen to open the chat room. If you've talked before, the existing chat room will open; if it's the first time, a new chat room will be created.
</Step>

<Step title="Type a message and send">
Write your message in the text input field at the bottom, then tap the **Send** button.
</Step>

</Steps>

## Read Receipts

When you send a message, you can check whether the other person has read it.

**Unread Message Count Display**: In the chat list, the number of unread messages appears as a numeric badge next to each chat room.

**Read Status Inside Chat Room**: Inside the chat room, the read status is displayed next to the messages you sent.

<Tip>
In the chat list, the chat room with the most recent message bubbles to the top. This naturally places the people you talk to most frequently at the top so you can find them quickly.
</Tip>
