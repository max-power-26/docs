## What is ClawTalk?

ClawTalk is a **business messenger**. You can comfortably chat with colleagues just like popular messengers, and you can also delegate tasks to the AI bot.

It can be used directly from your smartphone browser and operates as a PWA without needing app installation.

## Screen Layout

ClawTalk has **4 tabs** at the bottom.

<CardGroup cols={2}>

<Card title="Friends" icon="user-group">
Check and manage your friends list. They are organized into favorites and regular friends. You can tap a friend's profile to start a 1:1 chat immediately.
</Card>

<Card title="Chats" icon="comments">
Shows the list of ongoing chats. Both 1:1 chats and group chats are displayed, and the number of unread messages is shown as a badge.
</Card>

<Card title="Search" icon="magnifying-glass">
You can search for users by their email to add them as friends. This tab is also used when searching past chat content.
</Card>

<Card title="Settings" icon="gear">
You can modify your profile, configure notifications, log out, and more.
</Card>

</CardGroup>

## Core Features

<CardGroup cols={2}>

<Card title="1:1 Chat" icon="message">
Chat directly with friends. You can send and receive not only text but also photos, files, and voice messages.
</Card>

<Card title="Group Chat" icon="people-group">
You can create group chat rooms where multiple people can converse together. It's convenient for team communication.
</Card>

<Card title="Photos / Files / Voice" icon="paperclip">
Easily share photos and files, and use voice messages so you don't have to type long texts.
</Card>

<Card title="Veta AI Bot" icon="robot">
You can converse with the AI bot Veta via chat. Delegate tasks like asking work questions, organizing data, checking schedules, and more.
</Card>

</CardGroup>

## Read Receipts and Notifications

In ClawTalk, you can check whether the other person has read your message after sending it. The number of unread messages is displayed as a number in the chat list, and when a new message arrives, a **Push notification** is sent to your smartphone.

<Tip>
You can delegate tasks to the Veta AI bot via chat. For example, if you say "Tell me today's schedule" or "Summarize this content," you will receive an immediate response. Please refer to the Veta AI Bot section for more details.
</Tip>
