## Sending Photos

You can easily share photos in a chat. The photos you send are automatically resized to an appropriate size and transmitted quickly.

<Steps>

<Step title="Tap the paperclip icon in the chat room">
Tap the **paperclip icon** on the left side of the message input field.
</Step>

<Step title="Select an image">
Choose **Image** from the attachment menu, then pick a photo from your gallery to send. You can also take a photo right away with your camera.
</Step>

<Step title="Send">
After checking the preview of the selected photo, tap the **Send** button.
</Step>

</Steps>

**Full-Screen Image View**: Tap a received image in the chat room to view it full screen. You can also zoom in and out with two fingers.

## Sending Files

You can share various files via chat, such as documents, Excel sheets, PDFs, and more.

<Steps>

<Step title="Tap the paperclip icon in the chat room">
Tap the **paperclip icon** on the left side of the message input field.
</Step>

<Step title="Select a file">
Choose **File** from the attachment menu, then select the file you want to send.
</Step>

<Step title="Send">
Check the file name and size, then tap the **Send** button.
</Step>

</Steps>

<Warning>
Files sizes up to **50MB** are supported. Files exceeding 50MB cannot be sent, so please use Google Drive or similar services for larger files.
</Warning>

## Sending Voice Messages

When it's hard to type a long text, you can use voice messages.

<Steps>

<Step title="Press and hold the microphone button">
Press and hold the **microphone button** on the right side of the message input field with your finger to start recording.
</Step>

<Step title="Release your finger after speaking">
Release your finger after saying what you want to deliver; the recording will stop and be sent automatically.
</Step>

</Steps>

<Tip>
If you want to cancel the recording while holding, slide your finger to the left. The recording will be canceled and the message will not be sent.
</Tip>

<Note>
When sending a voice message for the first time, the browser will request microphone access permission. You must select **Allow** to use voice messages.
</Note>
