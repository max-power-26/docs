## What is Group Chat?

Group chat is a feature where **multiple people converse together in one chat room**. It can be utilized for various purposes, such as team meetings, project discussions, and department announcements.

## Creating a Group

<Steps>

<Step title="Tap the Create Group button in the Friends tab">
Navigate to the **Friends** tab from the bottom navigation, then tap the **Create Group** button at the top.
</Step>

<Step title="Select members">
Select the members you want to invite to the group from your friends list. You can select multiple people at once.
</Step>

<Step title="Set a group name">
Enter a name for the group chat room. It is recommended to set it as a team name or project name so it's easy to find later.
</Step>

<Step title="Tap the Complete button">
Once the setup is finished, tap the **Complete** button. The group chat room will be created, and you can start talking right away.
</Step>

</Steps>

## Managing Members

The person who creates the group becomes the **Admin**, and only the Admin can manage the members.

**Inviting Members**: Tap the settings icon at the top of the group chat room, then select **Invite Member**. You can choose members to add from your friends list.

**Kicking Members**: Check the member list in the group settings and select the member to be kicked out. Only the Admin can kick members out.

<Warning>
Kicked members cannot re-enter that group chat room unless the Admin invites them back.
</Warning>

## Leaving a Group

You can leave groups you no longer wish to participate in.

<Steps>

<Step title="Tap the Settings icon at the top of the group chat room">
Enter the group chat room you wish to leave, then tap the Settings icon at the top.
</Step>

<Step title="Select Leave Group">
Tap **Leave Group** from the settings menu.
</Step>

<Step title="Tap Confirm">
When the leave confirmation pop-up appears, tap **Confirm**. Once you leave, you will no longer receive messages from that group.
</Step>

</Steps>

<Note>
If the Admin leaves the group, one of the remaining members automatically becomes the new Admin. When the last member leaves, the group chat room is deleted.
</Note>

<Tip>
The group chat room name can be changed by the Admin even after creation. You can modify it by tapping the name in the group settings.
</Tip>
