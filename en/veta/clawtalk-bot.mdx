## What is the Veta AI Bot?

Veta is an **AI text bot** that operates within ClawTalk. Just like sending a message to a friend in a messenger app, sending a message to Veta allows it to handle tasks on your behalf.

You can use **29 different tools**—such as checking emails, managing schedules, searching files, and web searching—and it remembers the context of the conversation for up to 30 turns so you can seamlessly continue talking.

## Starting a 1:1 Chat with Veta

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Friends tab">
    Tap the **Friends** tab at the bottom of ClawTalk.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Veta">
    The **Veta** bot will be displayed at the top of your friends list. Please tap on Veta's profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Chatting">
    Tap the **Chat** button on the profile screen to start a 1:1 conversation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send a message">
    Type naturally as you usually speak. For instance, if you send "Tell me my schedule for this week," Veta will check your Google Calendar and summarize your schedule.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Inviting Veta to a Group Chat

You can also utilize Veta in team chat rooms.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a Group Chat Room">
    Create a new group chat from the Chat tab or open an existing group chat room.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite Veta">
    From the add member screen, select **Veta** to invite it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Talk to Veta in the Group">
    Just send a message in the chat room and Veta will respond. While conversing with your team members, you can request things like, "Veta, schedule a meeting for tomorrow."
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Conversation Example

If you send "Tell me my schedule for this week" to Veta, it works like this:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Send Message">
    Type "Tell me my schedule for this week" into the chat window.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Processing Indicator">
    When Veta receives the message, a **blinking three-dot animation** will appear. This means Veta is processing your request.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the Response">
    After a moment, Veta will organize and display your Google Calendar schedule for this week by date.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Thinking Animation

When you send a message to Veta, a **blinking three dots (...) animation** will appear.

This signifies that Veta is understanding your request, executing the necessary tools, and preparing a response. More complex tasks may take a little longer, so please wait a moment while the animation is visible.

<Warning>
  To use Google-related tools (Email, Calendar, Files, etc.), you must first connect your Google Account in the **ClawOnPhone app**. Open ClawOnPhone and press the **G Button** on the screen to connect it.
</Warning>

## Tips for Using Veta in Groups

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Real-time Support During Meetings" icon="users">
    While having a meeting in a team chat room, you can instantly ask Veta to search for materials, check schedules, or send emails. There is no need to switch to another app.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Share Information with Teammates" icon="share">
    Veta's responses are visible to all members of the group chat room. You can naturally share search results or schedule information with the whole team.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Task Instructions and Verification" icon="clipboard-check">
    If you ask, "What's on the schedule today?", your teammates can also see it, making it easier to coordinate work schedules.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Maintain Conversation Context" icon="brain">
    Veta remembers up to 30 turns of the conversation. Based on the previous chat, you can seamlessly ask follow-up questions or make additional requests.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  There's no need to use stiff commands with Veta. Try speaking naturally, like "Could you check my email?" or "What's happening tomorrow?"
</Tip>
