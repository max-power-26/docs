## Talking to Veta

The core feature of ClawOnPhone is **voice conversation**. By speaking to the AI assistant Veta, you can handle your tasks naturally as if you were talking to a person.

What sets it apart from other AIs is that you can **ask questions and confirm things in the middle of a process**. Veta doesn't just execute a one-off command; it accurately completes the task while continuing the conversation.

## Starting a Conversation

<Steps>

<Step title="Tap the Start button">
Press the **Start** button at the bottom of the screen to begin connecting with Veta. The sphere animation in the center of the screen will start moving.
</Step>

<Step title="Speak when the sphere turns green">
When the sphere **glows green**, it means Veta is listening. Please speak freely about what you want. For example, you can say, "Tell me my schedule for today."
</Step>

<Step title="Veta responds with voice">
Veta understands your request and answers with a voice. It may also ask follow-up questions if necessary. The conversation is also displayed as text on the screen.
</Step>

<Step title="Continue the conversation">
After hearing Veta's response, you can continue by stating additional requests or modifications. The conversation flows naturally without interruption.
</Step>

<Step title="Tap the Stop button to end the conversation">
To end the conversation, press the **Stop** button. The connection will close and the sphere will return to its standby state.
</Step>

</Steps>

## Typing Text

In situations where voice conversation is difficult, **text input** is also available.

Type your request into the Command Palette input field at the bottom of the screen, and Veta will process it in the exact same way. This is useful when you are in a meeting or a noisy environment.

## Multi-turn Conversation Example

Conversations with Veta don't have to end in one go. You can easily achieve your desired outcome through multiple back-and-forths.

<Steps>

<Step title="First Request">
**Me**: "Tell me my schedule for tomorrow."

**Veta**: "Here is your schedule for tomorrow, February 24th. You have a team meeting at 10 AM and a client visit at 2 PM."
</Step>

<Step title="Follow-up Request">
**Me**: "Add a schedule to write a report at 4 PM."

**Veta**: "Shall I add a schedule to 'Write a report' at 4 PM tomorrow?"
</Step>

<Step title="Confirmation and Completion">
**Me**: "Yes, add it."

**Veta**: "I have added the 'Write a report' schedule for 4 PM tomorrow."
</Step>

</Steps>

## Speech Recognition Tips

<Tip>
Using the app in a quiet environment greatly improves speech recognition. In noisy places, try using text input.
</Tip>

<Tip>
Speaking briefly and clearly helps Veta understand you more accurately. It's better to be specific, like "Send the quote email to Manager Kim at Taejun Pharmaceuticals" rather than simply "Send an email."
</Tip>

<Tip>
If Veta misunderstands, just say "No, try again," and it will re-process your request.
</Tip>
