## What are Push Notifications?

By using ClawOnPhone's Push notifications, you can receive important alerts **without having to open the app**. If the Watch feature spots a new email, or a calendar reminder triggers, you'll get a notification straight to your smartphone.

## Setting Up Notifications

<Steps>

<Step title="Launch the app for the first time">
When you first run ClawOnPhone, a pop-up prompt to **Allow Notifications** will appear.
</Step>

<Step title="Select Allow">
Tap the **"Allow"** button on the pop-up. You must allow this permission to receive Push notifications.
</Step>

<Step title="Notifications are activated">
Once allowed, you can receive Watch results, schedule reminders, and email alerts in real-time.
</Step>

</Steps>

## Differences between Android and iPhone

The method for setting up notifications differs slightly between Android and iPhone.

<CardGroup cols={2}>

<Card title="Android" icon="android">
You can receive Push notifications directly from the Chrome browser or the installed app.

Simply select "Allow" when the notification permission pop-up appears in your browser.
</Card>

<Card title="iPhone" icon="apple">
To receive Push notifications on an iPhone, you must **add the app to your Home Screen**.

Just leaving the website open in Safari will not let you receive notifications.
</Card>

</CardGroup>

<Warning>
iPhone users must install the app using "Add to Home Screen" in Safari and launch it from the home screen icon for Push notifications to work. You will not receive notifications if you open it directly in the Safari browser.
</Warning>

## Enabling Notifications on iPhone

<Steps>

<Step title="Open the app URL in Safari">
Go to the ClawOnPhone URL (https://alarm-e59f0.web.app) using Safari.
</Step>

<Step title="Add to Home Screen">
Tap the Share button at the bottom and select **"Add to Home Screen"**.
</Step>

<Step title="Launch the app from the Home Screen">
Launch the app by tapping the icon created on your Home Screen.
</Step>

<Step title="Select Allow on the notification pop-up">
When you launch the app, a pop-up asking for notification permission will appear. Tap "Allow" to complete the setup.
</Step>

</Steps>

## Turning Notifications On/Off

If you want to temporarily turn off notifications, you can adjust this in your device settings.

<CardGroup cols={2}>

<Card title="Turn off on Android" icon="gear">
Go to the Settings App → Notifications → ClawOnPhone (or Chrome) → change 'Allow notifications' to OFF.

You can switch it to ON whenever you want to receive them again.
</Card>

<Card title="Turn off on iPhone" icon="gear">
Go to the Settings App → Notifications → ClawOnPhone → change 'Allow notifications' to OFF.

You can switch it to ON whenever you want to receive them again.
</Card>

</CardGroup>

<Tip>
It is much more useful when paired with the Watch feature. If you set something like "Tell me today's schedule at 9 AM every morning," you will automatically receive a schedule alert every morning without opening the app.
</Tip>

<Note>
If you previously blocked notifications and want to allow them again, you must manually change the notification permissions for the browser or the app in your device's Settings app.
</Note>
