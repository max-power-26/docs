## What is Watch?

Watch is a feature where the **AI automatically performs repetitive tasks** once you instruct it. Set it up once, and Veta will periodically execute the task and notify you of the results via Push notifications.

You no longer need to repeat the same request; just assign it and forget about it.

## Setting Up Watch

When you request a repetitive task from Veta, a Watch is automatically created.

<Steps>

<Step title="Request a repetitive task from Veta">
Use your voice to suggest the task and the frequency you'd like it repeated.

**Speaking Examples**

- "Tell me my schedule for today every morning at 7 AM."
- "Let me know when a new email arrives."
- "Check customer emails every 5 minutes and suggest draft replies."
</Step>

<Step title="Veta registers the Watch">
After confirming your request, Veta registers the automatic execution. Once registered, it will send you a confirmation message.
</Step>

<Step title="It executes automatically">
The task will automatically be executed based on your set schedule. If there are new results, it will notify you immediately via Push notifications.
</Step>

</Steps>

## Managing Watch

You can view and cancel registered Watches at any time.

**Viewing the Watch list**: Tell Veta, "Show me the list of registered Watches," and it will display all currently active Watches.

**Deleting a Watch**: If you no longer need a Watch, just say "Delete the email checking Watch" and it will be removed.

<Tip>
"Assign and forget" is the core principle of Watch. The AI keeps working and only nudges you with a Push notification when necessary. Reduce the time you spend on repetitive checking tasks and focus on important work.
</Tip>

<Note>
Since Watch is executed on the server, it will work perfectly even if your smartphone screen is off. To receive Push notifications, you must grant notification permissions in your browser or app settings.
</Note>
