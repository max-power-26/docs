description: "Guides you on how to check past conversation sessions with Veta, continue conversations, or delete them."
---

## What is Session History?

Every conversation you have with Veta is **automatically saved**. What you say, Veta's responses, and the results of executed tasks remain in the history so you can review them at any time.

## Checking Conversation History

<Steps>

<Step title="Go to the History screen">
Tap the **History** menu on the home screen, and a list of past conversation sessions will appear.
</Step>

<Step title="Check the session list">
The most recent conversations are displayed at the top. Each session includes the date, time, and a summary of the conversation.
</Step>

<Step title="Tap a session to see details">
Tap on the session you want to view to see the full content of that conversation.
</Step>

</Steps>

## Continuing a Conversation

You can continue from a past conversation. Since Veta remembers the context of previous chats, the conversation flows naturally without breaking off.

<Steps>

<Step title="Select the session to continue">
Tap the desired session from the History list.
</Step>

<Step title="Review the conversation">
The previous conversation will be shown. You can check what Veta's last action was.
</Step>

<Step title="Press Start to resume">
Tap the Start button at the bottom to resume talking to Veta. Since Veta is aware of the previous context, you can seamlessly follow up with commands like "Show me that email again."
</Step>

</Steps>

<Tip>
The 'Continue Conversation' feature is useful when you want to divide a task across multiple sessions. For instance, you can finish drafting an email in the afternoon that you started in the morning.
</Tip>

## Deleting a Session

You can delete conversation records that you no longer need.

<Steps>

<Step title="Select the session to delete">
Find the session you wish to delete from the History list.
</Step>

<Step title="Tap the delete button">
On the session details screen, press the **Delete** button to erase that conversation record.
</Step>

</Steps>

<Warning>
Deleted conversation records cannot be recovered. If the session contains important information, please double-check before deleting.
</Warning>

## Recent Sessions on the Home Screen

Recent conversation sessions are displayed as cards at the bottom of the home screen. You can immediately continue a recent conversation without having to navigate to the History screen.

<Note>
Conversation logs are securely stored in your account and cannot be viewed by anyone else. You can access the same conversation history no matter which device you log in from.
</Note>
