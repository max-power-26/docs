## Searching Contacts and Sending Texts via Voice

By talking to Veta, you can find people in your Google Contacts or send SMS text messages. You can handle this entirely by voice without scrolling through your phone book or opening your messaging app.

<Note>
Searching for contacts requires **Google Account Connection**. Sending SMS messages, however, can be done without a Google connection as long as you provide the phone number.
</Note>

## Searching Contacts

You can search for people saved in your Google Contacts by their name, company, etc.

**Speaking Examples**

<CardGroup cols={2}>

<Card title="Search by Name" icon="address-book">
**"Find Manager Kim's contact."**

**"Tell me Director Lee's phone number."**
</Card>

<Card title="Search by Company" icon="building">
**"Find the contact for Taejun Pharmaceuticals."**

**"Show me the contact for the person in charge at Hanmi Pharmaceuticals."**
</Card>

</CardGroup>

Veta will find and read out the name, phone number, and email address. If multiple people are found, it will present them as a list.

## Sending SMS Messages

You can send SMS text messages using your voice. Simply state the recipient's name or phone number, along with the message you want to send.

**Speaking Examples**

<CardGroup cols={2}>

<Card title="Send Text by Name" icon="comment-sms">
**"Send a text to Director Lee saying 'I have arrived'."**

**"Send a text to Manager Kim saying 'The meeting is in 10 minutes'."**
</Card>

<Card title="Send Text by Number" icon="mobile">
**"Send a text saying 'Confirmed' to 010-1234-5678."**
</Card>

</CardGroup>

## Conversational Texting

You don't need to say all the information at once; Veta will ask for whatever details are missing.

<Steps>

<Step title="Request to send a text">
**Me**: "Send a text to Director Lee."

**Veta**: "What message would you like to send?"
</Step>

<Step title="Provide the message content">
**Me**: "Tell him the meeting location has changed to the 3rd-floor conference room."

**Veta**: "Shall I send the text 'The meeting location has changed to the 3rd-floor conference room' to Director Lee?"
</Step>

<Step title="Confirm and send">
**Me**: "Yes, send it."

**Veta**: "The text message has been sent to Director Lee."
</Step>

</Steps>

<Tip>
If you speak the name of someone saved in your contacts, Veta will automatically find their phone number and send the text. You don't need to recite the phone number yourself.
</Tip>

<Tip>
Veta will always ask you to confirm the message content before sending. This prevents you from accidentally sending incorrect information.
</Tip>
