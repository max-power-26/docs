## What is PC Node?

PC Node is a **dedicated app installed on your PC**. Once PC Node is installed, you can remotely control your PC using voice commands from your smartphone.

Whether you're moving between meetings or away from your desk, just speak into your phone and the task will be executed on your PC.

## Installation and Connection

<Steps>

<Step title="Install the ClawOnPhone PC app on your PC">
Download and install the Electron app for PC. It is supported on both Windows and Mac.
</Step>

<Step title="Log in with the same Google account on the PC app">
Log in to the PC app using the exact same Google account as the ClawOnPhone app on your phone. They must be the same account for the phone and PC to connect automatically.
</Step>

<Step title="Make sure the PC app is running">
The PC app appears as an icon in your system tray (taskbar). If you can see the icon, it is ready to be connected.
</Step>

</Steps>

<Warning>
If the PC Node app is not running, remote control will not work. To use your PC remotely, please keep the app open and running at all times.
</Warning>

## Available Commands

When you speak to Veta on your smartphone, the corresponding task will be executed on your PC immediately.

<CardGroup cols={2}>

<Card title="Open Website" icon="globe">
You can open any desired website on the PC Chrome browser.

**Speaking Examples**

- "Open Naver."
- "Open Google Drive."
- "Open the client's homepage."
</Card>

<Card title="Launch App" icon="window-restore">
You can launch programs installed on your PC.

**Speaking Examples**

- "Open VS Code."
- "Open Excel."
- "Open KakaoTalk."
</Card>

<Card title="Screen Capture" icon="camera">
You can capture your PC screen and view it instantly on your smartphone.

**Speaking Examples**

- "Capture the screen."
- "Show me my PC screen right now."
- "Take a screenshot."
</Card>

<Card title="Lock PC" icon="lock">
You can remotely lock your PC when you are away from your desk.

**Speaking Examples**

- "Lock the screen."
- "Lock my PC."
</Card>

</CardGroup>

<CardGroup cols={2}>

<Card title="Find Files" icon="file-magnifying-glass">
You can search for files saved on your PC.

**Speaking Examples**

- "Find the quote file."
- "Find the most recent Excel file."
- "Search for the report file."
</Card>

<Card title="Open Folder" icon="folder-open">
You can open specific folders on your PC via the File Explorer (or Finder).

**Speaking Examples**

- "Open the Downloads folder."
- "Open the Documents folder."
- "Open the Desktop."
</Card>

</CardGroup>

## Usage Examples

Here are some situations where it comes in handy:

**Checking files while moving around**: "Find the quote file on my PC and capture the screen."

**Meeting preparation**: "Open VS Code and launch the project folder."

**Organizing before leaving work**: "Lock the screen."

<Tip>
You can seamlessly combine PC remote control with Google Workspace features. For example, you can chain multiple tasks together like, "Check my email and save the attachments to my PC."
</Tip>

<Note>
Your PC and smartphone must be logged in with the exact same Google account. If the accounts are different, they will not connect, so please double-check.
</Note>
